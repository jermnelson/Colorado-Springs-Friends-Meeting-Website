{# START Testimonies #} Testimonies

We, as Quakers, do not affrim any specific doctrine or creed but instead try to live in accordance with the following testimonies:

  • Simplicity
  • Peace
  • Integrity
  • Community
  • Equality
{# END Testimonies #} Meetings {# START Meeting #}

Our Quaker Communtity centers around formal gathering of Friends. Our weekly Meeting for Worship is usually 10 am on First Day (Sunday). Our monthly Business Meeting, where consensus decisions are made concerning the financial, spritual, and physical matters is usually held the third first day after the close of Meeting for Worship.

  • Worship
  • Business
