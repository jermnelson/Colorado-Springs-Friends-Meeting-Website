    Our testimonies

    Our testimonies grew out of Friends’ experience of the Light of God in private contemplation and in worship together. These testimonies form one of the most powerful expressions of our faith to the world.

    We do not hold a specific creed, nor is there a statement of faith to which we are asked to subscribe. Instead, we hold a central belief that there is that of God within each person, and that we are called to act in recognition of that Divine presence in ourselves and others.

    The conviction to honor ‘that of God in each one’ can lead to great diversity among Friends. We hold these testimonies in common
